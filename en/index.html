Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my data if I uninstall the extension?

Your tabs remain as Chrome bookmarks, so your links are preserved even after uninstalling. When you reinstall, your groups and tabs are restored.
※ Theme, color, and tag settings are stored locally and will be reset on removal.

Is PRO a subscription?

No, it's a one-time purchase. Currently on sale for $4.99 (regular $9.99). Pay once, use forever.

Which browsers are supported?

Google Chrome 114+ and Microsoft Edge (Windows / Mac / Linux / ChromeOS).

Is my data sent to external servers?

Tab and group data is stored locally in your browser. Only an anonymous ID is used for license verification. Some external services are used for favicon display and search. See our Privacy Policy →

Can I restore tabs after a PC restart?

Yes. Tabs managed by LB TAB Layers are saved as Chrome bookmarks, so they can be restored with one click from the side panel after any restart, crash, or forced shutdown.

What's the difference between Free and PRO?

Free includes basic tab management and bookmark saving. PRO (one-time purchase) unlocks all 40 themes, AI search, focus timer, and all features.
